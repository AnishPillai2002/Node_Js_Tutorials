About Me

I am passionate about web development and have been working in the field for several years. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I specialize in building modern, responsive websites and web applications.

Throughout my career, I have gained experience in various front-end frameworks, such as React and Angular, and have worked extensively with back-end technologies like Node.js and Express. I enjoy combining my technical skills with a keen eye for design to create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces.

In addition to my technical expertise, I am a great collaborator and enjoy working in a team environment. I thrive on solving complex problems and take pride in writing clean, efficient code. Continuous learning is important to me, and I stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new hiking trails, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or playing guitar. I believe that a well-rounded life outside of work contributes to personal growth and creativity.
